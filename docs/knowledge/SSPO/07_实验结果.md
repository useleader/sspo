3. **领域泛化**: 在医疗(UltraMedical)和商业(DSP)数据集上同样有效

## 7.3 Ablation Studies

### Prior Sensitivity (10% UltraFeedback)

| Prior | Phi-2 LC | Mistral LC | Llama3 LC |
|-------|----------|------------|-----------|
| 0.1 | 6.8 | 25.6 | 17.8 |
| 0.3 | 7.3 | 29.0 | 19.9 |
| **0.5** | **7.7** | **30.0** | **20.7** |
| 0.7 | 7.6 | 28.8 | 20.7 |
| 0.9 | 7.4 | 25.7 | 19.5 |

**发现**: prior=0.5最优，但其他值也保持竞争力。

### Adaptive Scheduling的消融

| Size | Scheduler | Phi-2 LC | Mistral LC | Llama3 LC |
|------|-----------|----------|------------|-----------|
| 1% | ✓ (adaptive) | **7.2** | **26.7** | **15.0** |
| 1% | ✗ (γ'=0.1) | 6.5 | 24.1 | 13.2 |
| 1% | ✗ (γ'=0.5) | 6.8 | 26.0 | 14.1 |
| 10% | ✓ (adaptive) | **7.7** | **30.0** | **20.7** |

**发现**: Adaptive scheduler对性能提升至关重要。

## 7.5 Case Study: 隐式偏好挖掘

**Question**: "Can you help me figure out how to sew a button on a shirt?"

**KTO回答**: "You can just take a button and a shirt and then sew it on."

**SSPO回答**: "Absolutely! Here's a step-by-step guide: 1. Gather your supplies: button, thread, needle, thimble, scissors. 2. Thread your needle..."

**分析**: SSPO从UltraChat的unpaired data中学到了详细的步骤格式，即使question与paired data相似度低，也能从unpaired data中提取有用模式。
